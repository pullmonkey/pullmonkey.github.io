When you are looking at a route in bikely, you can go to the “Show” tab in the upper-left of the map. Hover over the “Show” tab and you will see “Elevation Profile” as the second item in the drop down list. Click “Elevation Profile”. Then a modal window pops up and you should see a graph similar to the one in this post.

Hope that helps.
